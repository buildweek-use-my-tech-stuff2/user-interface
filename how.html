How Do I Find The Gear I Need?

Search our marketplace for gear near you! Send over the deposit and the gear will be on it's way to you.

After your account is set up and ready to go you will see all gear posted in your area. You will see the associated deposit and all the information about the item. If it looks like what you are looking for click rent now, and you'll have the gear in your hands in no time!

How Do I Post My Gear?

Setting up an account with Rent Tech is easy!

Just sign up in your area and you can start posting your gear straight away. Here at Rent Tech we like you to be in control. You are able to set your own deposits and you'll be notified when someone has asked to rent your gear. You can then confirm the renter and meet up in person or send your gear off!
